Human Rights Issues in Asia
Published January 2020
Asian countries have demanded increasing international attention in the recent decades, from China's rise as an industrial powerhouse to Korea's ever-present influence in Western youth media. However, with more power inevitably comes more frequent - and more visible - abuses of human rights. In this issue, our contributers Daniel Botros, Francesco Rende, Elias Hammoud, Ibrahim Fadel, Nasrudeen Oladimeji, and Abdulsalam Oladimeji investigate various human rights violations in Asia and assess their greater impacts for the rest of the world.
