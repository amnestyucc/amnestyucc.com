UCC Shared Trail Trip to Vancouver and Hazelton

From June 21 to June 28 (2022), UCC is organizing a trip to British Columbia

The purpose of this trip is to provide students with an immersive experience and an opportunity to gain knowledge of Indigenous perspectives. After arriving in Vancouver, the group will fly to Hazelton in northern B.C. in the heart of the Gitxsan and Wet’suwet’en First Nations territories.

Cathy Busby, the artist who created our We Call installation, created a parallel installation in a school there, Gitskan Wet’suwet’en Education Society. She will meet the group and host the tour.

The group will then fly back to Vancouver to participate in the remainder of the Shared Trail program. They will travel by bus to Keremeos for three days and participate in workshops and guided reflection sessions to learn about Syilx history, culture, worldview, and contemporary perspectives.

Please contact Mr. Babits if you have any questions!
