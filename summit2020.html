Amnesty "Human Rights in the Times of COVID-19" Summit

While the vast majority of us are going to school during the pandemic, not everybody has been fortunate enough to live under as much stability. For many, the COVID-19 pandemic has only worsened human rights abuses. Domestic violence and violence against women rates are at an all-time high, China continues to hold the Uighur people in concentration camps, and many more human rights abuses rage on.

On Saturday, May 9th, we'll be hosting an online discussion-based summit from 1-2:30 pm where we'll discuss the pandemic's effect on human rights issues.
