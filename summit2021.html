Amnesty Summit 2021 - Truth and Reconciliation

On Saturday, May 1st, the Amnesty International Club will be hosting an online discussion-based summit from 9-11am on the issue of Truth and Reconciliation.

This year's summit topics include Canada's indigenous history, recent developments of Bill C-15 and the UN Declaration on Indigenous Peoples, and the impacts that century old policies continue to have on Canada todaay.

Please register using the google form below if you are interested. Both students and teachers are welcome!
